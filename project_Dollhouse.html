About the project:

Survival of the Cutest is developed in the style of old school survival horrors, within a much more miniature world. Set in a dollhouse, you play as a bunny rabbit doll as they flee from a relentless, dart-throwing action man. Players can sacrifice friends, complete quests, and make use of tools within the environment to ensure their escape.

Details

Sarge AI Brain.

The main enemy in the game, Sarge, is a simple AI using a behaviour tree with various branches deciding his behaviour. While the majority of Sarge's behaviour is dictated by the behaviour tree some of his abilities such as pawn sensing, special abilities to use and extra functionality is being stored and processed in the AI controller.

To create a somewhat advanced AI I created multiple custom decorators and services to help track variables from the behaviour tree blackboard and the AI controller.

More information will be added once the project reaches first playable demo stage!

Player controller and camera.

The player controller is very simple and effective. It allows the player to move in every direction as well as jump and crouch. With that the player can also lean left and right to peak around corners.

To make the player more immersed I have also added multiple camera shakes to simulate the head bop of walking, running, walking while crouching and idling.

The "Is There Wall Next To Camera" macro simply shoots a trace to the side of the camera and returns a bool depending on the trace hit.

More information will be added once the project reaches first playable demo stage!
