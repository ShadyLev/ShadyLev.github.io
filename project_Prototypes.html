Cave fishing

Cave environment made by team artists.

Spear gun made by team artists.

Fish AI made by me.

About the project:

This prototype was created alongside the Dollhouse game to decide what type of game our team wants to create. The idea was to create a cave fishing game in which the player would have to traverse a complex cave system to discover what happened to their now sunken world.

Engine:

Unreal Engine 5

Platform:

Windows

Team size:

12

Development time:

2 weeks

Download:

On request

What I did:

  • Player movement in water and land.
  • Fish AI in 3D space, with a behaviour tree but no pathfinding.
  • Custom pawn sensing.


Monster Camera

About the project:

I wanted to create a simple system in which the player can use a film camera to take pictures of the environment. The catch being that the camera can catch the glimpse of a invisible monster.
