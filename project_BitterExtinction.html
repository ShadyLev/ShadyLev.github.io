About the project:

Bitter Extinction is a tower defence shooter, with a dieselpunk aesthetic. You are commissioned with fortifying an abandoned mining colony. The catch is this glacial planet is home to metal devouring creatures, evolving as they feast on your defences. Extinction is the only way.

Engine:

Unity

Platform:

Windows

Team size:

8

Development time:

5 months


What I did:

  • UI design and functionality, including the main menu.
  • Custom key rebinding system.
  • Game settings menu.
  • Enemy pathfinding and AI.
  • Gun selection wheel and tower selection tool.
  • Round system.
  • Audio implementation.
  • Level design.
